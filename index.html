Projects

Book Vault

Also includes a search feature to find books registered in the system.

Smart Vault

Banking application that allows the user create an account in which they can deposit, checkout, and transfer money to other accounts. Also includes additional features such as a stock market simulation game, vouchers thay can be scanned, and a facial recognition system.

Waste management application that allows users to scan their meals to distinguish food items that contribute to waste and food items that do not. Based on the scan, the app also informs the user how they can reduce their waste. 3rd Place Hack49 Winner project.
